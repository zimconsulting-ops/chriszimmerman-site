Scope: tool stack · workflow mapping · consolidation paths

What I need from you

Lock these before the 72-hour clock starts. The tighter the intake, the tighter the read. You'll fill these on the next page when you start your scan.

Required

  • The question — one sentence, scoped as tight as you can get it
  • The decision this informs — what call are you about to make, and by when
  • The stakes — rough dollar amount in play ($10K range, $100K range, etc.)
  • Your context — role (solo operator / fractional exec / founder), industry, rough company size
  • What you've already checked — sources looked at, angles ruled out. Saves you buying research you already have.
  • What "done" looks like — a good answer for you is…? (go/no-go with three data points / vendor shortlist ranked by X / etc.)

Helpful · optional

  • Competitive or vendor set already in your head
  • Sources you trust or distrust (analyst firms, industry pubs, outlets you don't want cited)
