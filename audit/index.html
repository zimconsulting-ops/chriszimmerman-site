Common questions

FAQ.

Can't I just ask ChatGPT to audit my stack?
You can. It'll generate plausible-sounding recommendations based on patterns in its training data. Those recommendations won't account for your specific context, won't prioritize by leverage, and won't catch the things that aren't on the public web. The judgment is the product.
Why no call?
Calls are expensive (your time, my time, scheduling friction) and async work usually produces better recommendations. You have time to think about your intake answers. I have time to research properly. If you want a synchronous conversation, book a $89 Quick Answer instead — different product, different format.
What if I want you to implement the recommendations?
That's Custom Build ($549+) or Lane 1 Custom Tools ($1,950+). The audit is intentionally diagnostic, not implementation. Many clients audit first, then book implementation work for whichever 1-2 recommendations matter most.
Will my audit be shared publicly?
No. Audits are confidential by default. If you'd like to be a case study (with anonymization), that's opt-in and you get a discount on a follow-up engagement.
What if my stack is really simple?
Then the audit will be shorter — maybe 3 recommendations instead of 6 — and the focus will be on what to add or strengthen, not what to cut. The price doesn't change because the thinking work is the cost, not the page count.
What if my stack is really complex (15+ tools, multiple agents)?
Then the audit might prioritize 3 things to focus on rather than try to cover everything. If your situation is genuinely too broad for a 1-page audit, I'll tell you upfront and refund the $349. Better to set scope right than overpromise.
Refund policy?
Full refund if I haven't started work yet (within 24 hours of intake submission). Once I start research, no refund — but if you're unhappy with the deliverable, I'll do one round of revisions at no charge.
